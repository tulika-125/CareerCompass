Core Engineering Careers (PCM)

Detailed descriptions, degree paths, and roadmaps for core engineering careers suitable for PCM students.

Mechanical Engineer

What they do: Design, analyze, and maintain mechanical systems such as machines, engines, and HVAC systems.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Tech / B.E. Mechanical Engineering
  • Diploma → B.Tech (Lateral Entry)
  • M.Tech (Thermal / Design / Manufacturing)

Roadmap

  1. Engineering mechanics, thermodynamics, materials
  2. CAD/CAE tools (AutoCAD, SolidWorks)
  3. Manufacturing processes
  4. Plant & workshop internships
  5. Core industry / PSU / M.Tech

Roadmap

  5. Power sector / PSUs
