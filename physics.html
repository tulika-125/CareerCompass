Computer & Software Careers (PCM)

Clear role descriptions and a practical roadmap for Computer & Software career options suitable for PCM students.

Physicist

What they do: Study matter, energy, and the fundamental laws of nature through theory and experiments.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Physics
  • M.Sc Physics
  • PhD Physics (mandatory for research roles)

Roadmap

  1. Strong foundation in classical & modern physics
  2. Advanced mathematics
  3. Laboratory & computational skills
  4. Specialize during M.Sc
  5. PhD → Research scientist / academic

Astrophysicist

What they do: Study stars, galaxies, black holes, and the universe.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Physics
  • M.Sc Physics / Astrophysics
  • PhD Astrophysics

Roadmap

  1. Physics & mathematics
  2. Astronomy & cosmology
  3. Data analysis & simulations
  4. Research projects
  5. PhD → Space agencies / research institutes

Nuclear Scientist

What they do: Work with nuclear reactions, radiation, and atomic energy.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Physics
  • M.Sc Nuclear Physics
  • PhD Nuclear Science

Roadmap

  1. Nuclear & particle physics
  2. Radiation safety
  3. Reactor physics
  4. Research internships
  5. Nuclear labs / power sector

Medical Physicist

What they do: Apply physics in medical imaging and radiation therapy.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Physics
  • M.Sc Medical Physics
  • Clinical certification

Roadmap

  1. Radiation physics
  2. Medical imaging
  3. Safety protocols
  4. Hospital training
  5. Medical physicist role

Space Scientist

What they do: Research space systems, satellites, and planetary science.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Physics / Engineering
  • M.Sc Space Science
  • PhD (research roles)

Roadmap

  1. Physics & orbital mechanics
  2. Space systems
  3. Programming & simulations
  4. Space research projects
  5. Space agencies (ISRO etc.)

Quantum Researcher

What they do: Research quantum mechanics, quantum computing, and quantum materials.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Physics
  • M.Sc Quantum Physics
  • PhD Quantum Science

Roadmap

  1. Advanced quantum mechanics
  2. Linear algebra & computation
  3. Quantum algorithms
  4. Research publications
  5. Academia / advanced R&D
