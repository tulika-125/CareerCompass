Chemistry Careers (PCM)

Clear role descriptions and structured roadmaps for Chemistry career options suitable for PCM students.

Chemist

What they do: Study substances, their composition, properties, and chemical reactions.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Chemistry
  • M.Sc Chemistry
  • PhD (for research roles)

Roadmap

  1. Build strong basics in organic, inorganic & physical chemistry
  2. Develop laboratory techniques
  3. Choose specialization in M.Sc
  4. Complete research projects
  5. Industry or research career

Analytical Chemist

What they do: Analyze chemical composition and quality of materials.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Chemistry
  • M.Sc Analytical Chemistry

Roadmap

  1. Analytical chemistry fundamentals
  2. Learn instruments (HPLC, GC, UV, MS)
  3. Data interpretation skills
  4. Quality control lab training
  5. Pharma / chemical industry

Organic Chemist

What they do: Work with carbon-based compounds and chemical synthesis.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Chemistry
  • M.Sc Organic Chemistry
  • PhD (R&D roles)

Roadmap

  1. Reaction mechanisms
  2. Organic synthesis techniques
  3. Spectroscopy (NMR, IR, MS)
  4. Research lab experience
  5. Pharma / R&D companies

Inorganic Chemist

What they do: Study metals, minerals, and inorganic compounds.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Chemistry
  • M.Sc Inorganic Chemistry

Roadmap

  1. Coordination chemistry
  2. Solid-state chemistry
  3. Material characterization
  4. Lab-based research
  5. Industry or academia

Physical Chemist

What they do: Apply physics principles to chemical systems.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Chemistry
  • M.Sc Physical Chemistry

Roadmap

  1. Thermodynamics & kinetics
  2. Quantum chemistry
  3. Computational chemistry tools
  4. Research projects
  5. Academic / industrial research

Polymer Scientist

What they do: Design and analyze polymers and plastic materials.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Chemistry
  • M.Sc Polymer Science

Roadmap

  1. Polymer chemistry basics
  2. Processing techniques
  3. Testing & quality control
  4. Industrial training
  5. Polymer & plastics industry

Environmental Chemist

What they do: Study chemical effects on the environment and pollution control.

Degree / Education Path

  • B.Sc Chemistry
  • M.Sc Environmental Chemistry

Roadmap

  1. Environmental chemistry
  2. Pollution analysis
  3. Waste treatment methods
  4. Field & lab projects
  5. Environmental labs / NGOs
